About Lumina

Our Mission

At Lumina, our mission is to illuminate the path for collaborative study sessions at UW-Madison.

Our platform provides resources, tools, and support to help learners and educators connect, grow, and succeed.

What We Offer

  • Make group study accessible, inclusive, and easy to organize.
  • Foster a supportive and engaging learning community
  • Empower Students to take control of their academic success
